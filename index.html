VALYRIAN DRAFT

CAD Modeling Event

Forge Your Design. Unleash the Dragon. Prove your mastery in CAD modeling by creating precision 2D drawings and 3D models under competitive conditions.

📌 Rules & Regulations

  • Participants are allowed to use any CAD software of their choice
  • Participants are permitted to bring their own laptops for the event
  • ⏱️ Total duration of the event is 1.5 hours
  • The competition follows a continuous stage format:
Stage 1

Create a fully defined 2D drawing based on the problem provided by the organizing committee

Stage 2

Immediately proceed without any time gap to develop the 3D part model and assembly as instructed

  • ⚠️ Any form of malpractice or external assistance is strictly prohibited

The Ultimate Sales Pitch Challenge

If you can sell a disaster as a masterpiece, this stage is yours. Engineering logic is optional; confidence is mandatory. Your mission? Convince our panel of "Investors" that your product is the next global breakthrough.

0% Technical Knowledge required
100% Humor & Confidence
🏆 Epic prizes for the most hilarious pitches!

📌 Rules

  • 👥 Team Size: 1 to 3 members
  • 📵 No Phones Allowed: Use of mobile phones or the internet during prep leads to instant disqualification

A fun-filled and challenging team-based game designed to bring together fun, entertainment and teamwork. The event challenges participants through music, memory, movies and coordination based tasks.   • 👥 Each team should contain 2–4 participants
  • 🚫 Elimination will be based on their scores
